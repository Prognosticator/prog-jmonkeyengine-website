Simply enter the game room, walk around, sit at a table, and start playing Collect The 10s!

This game is focused on bringing recognition to the players in ONLINE play by offering players an ELO rating (similar to chess) when they play rated games, and getting a world and country ranking based on this rating.

Single player mode is also available where you play at different skill levels against CPU players in Ladder and Round Robin tournaments. The CPU uses a strong fine-tuned AI when set at the higher skill levels and is a very formidable opponent!
